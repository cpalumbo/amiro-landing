# Amiro Health

**AI employee to run patient journeys on autopilot**

## The Problem

Most small and medium clinics still manage patient journeys manually — follow-ups, rebookings, no-show recovery, retention, and upselling — all through email, messaging, spreadsheets, and staff coordination. Things fall through the cracks every day, costing clinics revenue and hurting patient outcomes.

## The Solution

Amiro is an AI employee that plugs into a clinic's existing tools and runs patient journeys on autopilot. Clinic directors simply ask what they need, and Amiro handles it — while proactively suggesting campaigns and workflows based on best practices from top-performing clinics.

Amiro gives every clinic the operational engine that only well-run chains can build today.

## How It Works

Amiro connects to a clinic's EMR and existing tools, then gives staff two ways to work with it:

### Chat Interface — The AI Employee

The primary way to use Amiro is through a conversational chat. Staff talk to Amiro like they would a colleague: ask what needs attention today, get recommendations on which campaigns to launch, surface patients who haven't rebooked, or flag overdue follow-ups. Amiro has full context from the clinic's EMR and tools, so it can act on what it knows — not just answer questions.

This is the interface designed for the reality of healthcare: most clinic staff don't have time for yet another SaaS dashboard. The chat lets them stay on top of operations quickly and productively, without learning a new tool.

### Web Application — Full Visibility and Control

For clinic directors or staff who want more detail, Amiro also offers a web application where they can see which campaigns are running, what patient flows are active, and how they're performing. Campaigns and workflows can be edited, fine-tuned, or built from scratch through the UI — giving full control when there's time to use it.

### Two Operating Modes

- **Autopilot mode** — Amiro runs campaigns and workflows fully autonomously. Results go out, follow-ups get triggered, no-shows get recovered — all without manual intervention.
- **Human-in-the-loop mode** — Amiro drafts every campaign and workflow, but a staff member reviews and approves each one before it goes live. This gives clinics full control while still offloading the work of creating and managing patient journeys.

### White-Label Platform

Every client gets a fully branded experience deployed on their own domain — a patient results portal (where patients access lab results and book their next visit via a secure link), a staff dashboard (daily tasks, follow-ups, operations queue), and lightweight staff portals (one-click task views sent via notification, email, or WhatsApp). Amiro handles onboarding, EMR integration, and DNS setup as part of a white-glove deployment.
